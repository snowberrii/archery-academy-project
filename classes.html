Classes

Group Lesson

Basic (A101)

MON & WED, 4-5 PM

A101 is a basic archery course that focuses on beginning archery safety, skills, and drills. Participants learn about archery form, range rules and etiquette. Form training and fun games are introduced in this class. Equipment will be provided with exception of safety kit.

Pre-requisite : Interest in learning archery. Minimum age 8 years old or 7 w/ pre-evaluation.

Price : $120 / month

Book Now

Intermediate (A102)

TUE & THU, 4-5 PM

A102 is an intermediate archery course focuses on safety, form, and introduces new styles of shooting Recurve, Compound bows. Fun games and form reinforcement techniques are emphasized. Equipment provided with exception of safety kit.

Pre-requisite : A101 or recommendation from private lesson instructor. Minimum age 7 years old.

Price : $120 / month

Book Now

Advance (A103)

TUE & THU, 5-6 PM

A103 focuses on a continued education towards tournament shooting styles used by archery associations worldwide. Participants receive guidance on shooting form and equipment selection. Equipment will be provided with exception of safety kit.

Pre-requisite : A101/A102 or equivalent. Minimum age 7 years old.

Price : $120 / month
